Core Philosophy
⚡

Performance

Non-essential services and background processes are cut from the root. Lower latency, less RAM overhead, more horsepower for what actually matters.

🔒

Privacy

Deep registry edits, host-level blocks, and systematic dismantling of Microsoft's telemetry stack. Your data stays yours — full stop.

🛡

Security

Attack Surface Reduction rules, exploit protection, and hardened authentication policies — security that doesn't take a back seat to speed.

What Gets Done

No bloat.
No nonsense.

01

Intelligent Debloating

Edge, OneDrive, Teams, and preinstalled UWP apps are removed properly — not just hidden.

02

Taskbar Minimalism

Auto-hide, centered, small icons, no clock or tray clutter. Your screen is your workspace.

03

Service Management

Error reporting, telemetry daemons, and background maintenance tasks are selectively disabled.

04

LTSC Edition Conversion

Genuine conversion to Windows Enterprise LTSC — the cleanest, most stable Windows available.

05

Classic Context Menu

Restores the Win10 right-click menu with power-user additions: Take Ownership, Open with Notepad.

06

Explorer Refinement

Compact mode, file extensions always visible, Home and Gallery sidebar sections removed.
