Core Philosophy

Performance

Non-essential services cut from the root. Lower latency, less overhead, more horsepower for what actually matters.

Privacy

Registry edits, host-level blocks, systematic dismantling of Microsoft's telemetry. Your data stays yours.

Security

ASR rules, exploit protection, hardened auth policies — security that doesn't take a back seat to speed.

What Gets Done

No bloat.
No noise.

Intelligent Debloating

Edge, OneDrive, Teams, and preinstalled UWP apps removed properly — not just hidden.

Taskbar Minimalism

Auto-hide, centered, small icons — no clock or tray clutter. Your screen is your workspace.

Service Management

Telemetry daemons and background tasks selectively disabled — not everything, just the waste.

LTSC Conversion

Genuine conversion to Windows Enterprise LTSC — the cleanest, most stable Windows available.

Classic Context Menu

Restores Win10 right-click with power additions: Take Ownership, Open with Notepad.

Explorer Refinement

Compact mode, extensions always visible, Home and Gallery sidebar removed.
